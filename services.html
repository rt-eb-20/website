Data Science Services

Data science and consulting for research teams, SMEs, and industry — from a first feasibility check to a model running in production.

I combine ten years of applied data science with academic rigour: NLP and machine learning built to work in practice, and honest advice on what is feasible, what it costs, and how to run the project. Work can be a focused analysis, hands-on development, or ongoing methodological support.

NLP & large language models

Text classification, information extraction, and summarisation; integrating LLM APIs (OpenAI, Google Gemini) into your pipelines, with prompt design and rigorous evaluation of model quality.

Prediction

Machine learning & analytics

Predictive models and customer analytics — churn, customer lifetime value, customer journey, segmentation and personas, recommendation — including deep neural networks where they earn their keep.

Authenticity

Enablement

Workshops & training

Talks and hands-on training on data science, NLP, and generative AI, drawing on years of university teaching and thesis supervision.

How I work

  • Start with the question. A short scoping conversation to clarify the goal, the data, and whether the effort is worth it.
  • Evidence over hype. Methods are chosen and evaluated on the merits; I say when a simpler approach — or none at all — is the right call.
  • Built to hand over. Documented, reproducible Python work you or your team can maintain, not a black box.
